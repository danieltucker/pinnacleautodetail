2
Set up Google Search Console
Tells Google your site exists and lets you track how it appears in search results
Required
Steps
  1. Go to search.google.com/search-console and sign in with your Google account.
  2. Click "Add property" and enter: pinnacleautodetail.com
  3. Choose the "URL prefix" option.
  4. Under "Other verification methods," select "HTML tag".
  5. You'll see a line of code that looks like:
    <meta name="google-site-verification" content="XXXXXXXXXXXXXXX">
  6. Copy that entire line and send it back.
  7. Do not click Verify yet — wait until after the tag has been added to the site.
After the tag is added, come back to Search Console and click Verify. Then go to Sitemaps and submit: https://pinnacleautodetail.com/sitemap.xml
Send back

The full <meta name="google-site-verification" ...> tag from step 5.
